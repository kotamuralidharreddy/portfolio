My Projects

Anti Sleep Alarm(Driver alert Device) :

•The main aim of an anti-sleep alarm using an IR sensor in spectacles is to detect when the wearer is becoming drowsy or falling asleep, and to alert them to wake up and stay alert. This type of system is particularly useful for drivers, as drowsy driving is a significant risk factor for accidents on the road. By detecting signs of drowsiness and alerting the driver to stay alert, an anti-sleep alarm can help to prevent accidents and keep the driver and others on the road safe.

Automatic vehicle accident detection system :

•The main aim of an automatic vehicle accident detection system using GPS, vibration sensors, and GSM is to provide real-time detection of accidents involving vehicles and notify relevant parties immediately. The system utilizes GPS to track the location of the vehicle and vibration sensors to detect any sudden changes in movement or impact force. This allows for quick response times and may help to reduce the severity of injuries sustained by the occupants of the vehicle.

Road Object Detection With Deep Learning :

•The main aim of road object detection with deep learning is to develop accurate and efficient systems that can automatically identify and locate objects on the road from images or video streams. This technology is a crucial component of various applications, particularly in the field of autonomous driving, advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), and traffic management.

Drowsiness Driver Alert System :

•The main goal of a drowsiness driver alert system is to prevent accidents caused by drowsy driving and promote road safety. By warning drivers about their drowsy state, they can take necessary breaks, rest, or hand over control to someone else, thus reducing the risk of potential accidents and injuries on the road. These systems play a crucial role in supporting drivers and making road travel safer for everyone.
